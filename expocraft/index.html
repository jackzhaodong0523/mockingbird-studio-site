My Story about
EXPO 2010

In 2010, the Shanghai World Expo was held, where artists and architects from all over the world converged in Shanghai to create a dazzling spectacle of global culture and creativity. Sadly, after the curtain fell on the World Expo, most of the pavilions in the Shanghai World Expo Park were torn down.

I was only a child then, and I never got to see that World Expo, which was the greatest sorrow of my childhood.

Ever since I first touched the sandbox game Minecraft in 2012, I have been wandering in the Minecraft world for more than a decade. I was captivated by the game mechanics of Minecraft, which gave me the freedom to build anything I wanted in the Minecraft world. Whether it was Chinese traditional architecture or modern architecture, I had designed and built countless buildings in Minecraft.

Now, I decided to make my childhood dream come true, and rebuild the 2010 Shanghai World Expo Park in the Minecraft world.
